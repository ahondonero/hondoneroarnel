What do we do?

In this time of pandemic crisis, we don't rely on the news anymore due to false information that is spreading in the internet especially in social media.

Don't you worry, this website 'Learn Davao' is a trustworthy website and we spread information that is reliable to the people of Davaoenos and we make sure that the data we provided is from the City Government.

Learn Davao will provide timely updates regarding the city's COVID-19 status, such as: Number of cases, High-risk areas, new implementations and, additional health tips and guide.

learn more

Who will benefit?

City Government

With The Existence Of Learn Davao, The Spread Of False Information Is Less One Thing For The Government To Worry About, It Will Help Increase The Awareness Of The People About The Current Situation.

City of Davao

The widespread use of Learn Davao will help the city in general, in a way that the wrong beliefs of the people about the vaccine and the virus may be corrected with a reliable information source.

Davaoenos

The people of Davao don't have to rely on facebook or any social media site for COVID-19 information anymore where false information is widespread, with Learn Davao all information are guaranteed reliable.
