Yaki-udon ₱390

a Japanese stir fry dish consisting of thick, smooth, white udon noodles mixed with a soy based sauce, meat, and vegetables.

Tsukemen ₱335

a Japanese noodle ramen dish in Japanese cuisine consisting of separate servings of noodles and soup or broth, whereby the noodles are dipped in the soup.

Nimono ₱265

a simmered dish in Japanese cuisine. A nimono generally consists of a base ingredient simmered in shiru stock flavored with sake, soy sauce, and a small amount of sweetening.

Miso Ramen ₱210

a Japanese noodle soup flavored with a paste made from fermented soy beans. Miso is one of three types of tare (seasoning) used to flavor ramen broth in Japan—the other two are shio (salt) and shoyu (soy sauce).

Nabemono ₱390

or simply nabe, is a variety of Japanese hot pot dishes, also known as one pot dishes and "things in a pot".

Ebi furai ₱255

a Japanese fried shrimp made with succulent shrimp coated with Panko breadcrumbs and fried till golden brown.
